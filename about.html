Project Description

The Laptop and Gadget Lending System is designed to help students and staff borrow digital devices efficiently. The system provides easy gadget booking, inventory tracking, secure user management, and technical support services.


Vision

To provide equal access to digital devices for all students and staff members.


Mission

To create a smart and efficient gadget lending environment with secure management.
